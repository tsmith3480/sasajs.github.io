About

This page is dedicated to a small group people of who listened to the Ross Tucker Even Money podcast and wanted to make their NFL Weekends a little more interesting. They formed a syndicate, each contributed $100 and began betting on the games.

The group was started by Steve Schilhabel, but the resident math major Tom Lee makes all the picks using the ELO model
